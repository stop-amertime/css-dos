This game has been tested in DOSBox 0.74 on a Raspberry Pi3 via Recalbox Version 18.02.09 

Installation instructions.

You can drop the .PC folder directly into the following folder on your Raspberry Pi:

\\RECALBOX\share\roms\dos\

Reboot the Pi.

The folder will be listed under the IBM tab in Recalbox.

Rename the folder

Enjoy the game on your big screen tv :)
